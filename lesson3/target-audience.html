Target Audience Personas

Mindful Mindy is a mom of four. She uses the information on weathermystorm.com to plan her outings. She makes sure to read the articles about heat exhaustion and frostbite so she knows how to dress her children.

Backpacker Barry likes to go hiking. He frequently checks for weather warnings so he isn't caught out in the rain. He likes to take pictures and scrolls through weathermystorm.com's Gallery page for inspiration.


Scenarios

I'm traveling from Soda Springs to Preston. Is it going to snow?

My child was playing outside in the middle of December. Is it cold enough for frostbite? What does frostbite look like?

I want to know what a nimbus cloud looks like.

A tornado touched down fifty miles away. How far can it travel? Where should I hide?

I love watching the sunrise. What conditions make the sunrise red? What about gold?
