Weathermystorm.com is a site dedicated to the weather.

Have you ever wanted to be able to give a weather report? Not as the meteorologist on television, but as yourself. Maybe you noticed that a storm has picked up, and you want to make sure people know so they can be safe. Guess what? You can! We accept weather reports from users passing through the area. Click the link below, or navigate to the Storm Center to fill out our quick and easy weather reports!
