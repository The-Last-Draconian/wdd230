Bonfire Night

On this day in history, Guy Fawkes tried to blow up Parliament. He was caught under the building, surrounded by gunpowder, holding a lit match. A few seconds later, and the entire building would have been blown up. Now, Bonfire Night is celebrated with fireworks and bonfires. Schoolchildren memorize a chant: Remember, remember, the fifth of November / The gunfire treason and plot.
